id: A unique numeric identifier for each star.

tyc: The stars identifier in the Tycho catalog.

gaia: The stars identifier in the Gaia catalog.

hyg: The stars identifier in the HYG database.

hip: The stars identifier in the Hipparcos catalog.

hd: The stars identifier in the Henry Draper catalog.

hr: The stars identifier in the Bright Star Catalog, also known as the Harvard Revised catalog.

gl: The stars identifier in the Gliese catalog of nearby stars.

bayer: The Bayer designation as a traditional name for the star.

flam: The Flamsteed designation of the star.

con: The constellation where the star is located.

proper: The proper name of the star.

ra: Right ascension of the star.

dec: Declination of the star.

pos_src: The source of the position data.

dist: The distance of the star from Earth.

x0, y0, z0: Cartesian coordinates of the star, with the Sun at the origin.

dist_src: The source of the distance data.

mag: The apparent magnitude of the star.

absmag: The absolute magnitude of the star.

ci: Color index of the star.

mag_src: The source of the magnitude data.

rv: Radial velocity of the star.

rv_src: The source of the radial velocity data.

pm_ra: Proper motion in right ascension.

pm_dec: Proper motion in declination.

pm_src: The source of the proper motion data.

vx, vy, vz: Space velocity components of the star.

spect: Spectral type of the star.

spect_src: The source of the spectral type data.
